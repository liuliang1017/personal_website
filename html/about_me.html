Research: Computational Fluid Dynamics

The research interest of Liang Liu is on how to improve the efficiency of simulations in computational fluid dynamics. A recent research work, instructed by Prof. Fidkowski, is a new combined adjoint approach, which uses output-based adjoint and entropy adjoint as two layers of indicators for mesh refinement. Meshes here are commonly used for spatial discretization and mesh refinement is a ususal approach to obtain accurate flow predictions by selectively reducing mesh size. Based on previous works, adjoints are shown to be a good indictor to reflect the sensitivity of output of interest to local residual (numerical error). However, the triditional adjoint approach is either containminated by furious entropy generation or affected by adjoint singularity. The new apporach only refines the region where output-based adjoint and entropy adjoint agree, which effectively avoid the waste of mesh allocation in uninteresting area and thus reduce the simulation cost.
